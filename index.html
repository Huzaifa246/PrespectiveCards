Width Prespective class

Prespective used to see our objects in 3D shape

Examples of prespective origin:
Top, bottom , right and left
perspective-origin: 500% 200%;
perspective-origin: x-position y-position;

Note: Both transform-style and perspective CSS properties are integral to 3D display and styling.
Transform-style determines whether child elements are positioned in 3D space or flattened within their parent's plane.
Perspective sets the depth of the 3D space, affecting the perception of transformed elements.
